site's Apply page sends them back to your DMs.

## Posting notes

**Order.** Lead with 01, then alternate proof and value: 02, 07, 05, 03, 08,
06, 04. Don't post the three case studies back to back - it reads as bragging.
Proof lands harder when something useful sits between each one.

**Cadence.** Two or three a week is plenty. Eight posts is roughly a month.

**Hashtags.** Instagram only - Facebook hashtags do close to nothing. Keep to
6-8 and rotate; identical tag blocks on every post get suppressed.

**Comments.** Reply to every one within the hour if you can. The engagement
matters, but more importantly it's the same behaviour we sell: a fast reply is
what turns interest into an inquiry.

**Sizes.** 1080x1080 works for the IG feed and FB. For Stories, place the
square on a plain `#1B1B1B` or `#FAF8F5` 1080x1920 background rather than
stretching it.

## Revised posting order

With the application posts in, lead with positioning and let proof follow:

**c01 → c08 (carousel) → c02 → c07 (carousel) → c05 → c03 → c06 → c04**

Open with the work montage - it shows range in one scroll and needs no
explanation. Then the intake carousel, so people know how you operate before
you start quoting numbers at them. After that, alternate: proof, value, proof.

Stories `c09` `c10` `c11` run alongside, not in sequence. Post one whenever a
feed post goes up so it appears in both places.

Carousels outperform single images on reach, so `c07` and `c08` are the two
worth boosting if you promote anything.
